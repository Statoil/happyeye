This is the employee happiness sensor.

You submit a feedback document logging happiness status, average, above average and below average. Submitting happiness feedback is "anonymous". We do not log any personal information, neither any cookies with information.

Frequency. You can submit feedback as often as you want, but twice a day is perhaps enough. We have no throttling mechanism avoiding anyone from hammering the system with feedback.

API.Submitting from other systems are available using a url:3000/happy with either get or post. Format is {happystatus: 'average'|'below'|'above'} Results are available to anyone (in the default setup) and exposed in Kibana at url:5601/

Source: https://github.com/Statoil/happyeye
